The [beyond the imitation game benchmark](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2206.04615.pdf). 
Refer to [BIG Bench Github](https://github.com/google/BIG-bench) and [[The Imitaion Game]].

There are 204 tasks in total. Task topics are diverse, drawing problems from linguistics, childhood development, math, common-sense reasoning, biology, physics, social bias, software development, and beyond. BIG-bench focuses on tasks that are believed to be beyond the capabilities of current language models.
